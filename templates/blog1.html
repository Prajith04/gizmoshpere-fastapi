iQOO Z9 Series

The iQOO brand has launched a trio of new devices aimed at the midrange segment:

iQOO Z9 Turbo

iQOO Z9 Turbo leads the pack with its iQOO 12-inspired design, Snapdragon 8s Gen 3 chipset, and 6,000 mAh battery with 80W fast charging. It features a 6.78-inch AMOLED display with FHD+ resolution and a 144Hz refresh rate.

Price:INR 23,133

iQOO Z9

iQOO Z9 features a Snapdragon 7s Gen 3 chip, 6,000 mAh battery with 80W fast charging, and a 6.78-inch AMOLED display with FHD+ resolution and a 144Hz refresh rate.

Price:INR 17,347

iQOO Z9x

iQOO Z9x is the affordable entry with Snapdragon 6 Gen 1 SoC, 44W charging, and a 6.72-inch IPS LCD with a 120Hz refresh rate.

Price:INR 15,032
